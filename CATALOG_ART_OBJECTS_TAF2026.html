Lizi Budagashvili

Tbilisi / New York · Oil on canvas and mixed media · 2022–2025

Born in Tbilisi (b. 2000), currently based in New York. MFA from the New York Academy of Art, BFA from Ringling College. Two-time Elizabeth Greenshields Foundation Grant recipient.

Exploring themes of childhood, memories, and nostalgia, my paintings capture the longing for simpler times by delving into the ephemeral, mirage-like recollections. I draw dreamscapes from memory gaps and create ethereal atmospheres through transparent veils.

SKNYPL

Modular sculpture / furniture · 200-year-old Georgian pine · Tbilisi

SKNYPL repurposes 200-year-old pine from a Georgian railway sawmill, cutting and reassembling the wood into modular sculptural furniture. Each piece carries the weight of industrial history — transformed into living objects that blur the line between sculpture and function, heritage and future.
